About Luxury Home

Our Luxury Home real estate agencies are located in the chic districts of Berges du Lac, our young and modern team offers exceptional real estate very close to Tunis and its airport. Come and discover these business and residential districts ideally located on the shores of Lake Tunis and very close to Tunis-Carthage airport, it has won over many large companies, embassies and businessmen. Our first Tunisian real estate agency set up in 2007 at Lac 2 des Berges du Lac in Tunis. This Tunisian neighborhood is experiencing an economic boom, and have become essential place for Tunisian business and development.
